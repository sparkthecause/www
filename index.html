This month we are connecting kids with tech

September is the height of back to school season, and to celebrate, we've partnered with Pepin Academies. For over fifteen years, Pepin Academies, a Hillsborough County Public Charter School, has helped hundreds of students with learning and related disabilities graduate high school, go to college, and find jobs. For students struggling with disabilities, these are not easy tasks. Like most schools, budgets are tight, so we want to ask for your help to empower these students with enhanced classroom technology.
