Spark the Cause has partnered with Mobex for September, read more below

During the month of September, Mobex is teaming up with Spark the Cause to enhance classroom technology at Pepin Academies, a tuition free charter school for kids with learning and related disabilities. Anyone that signs up for a new Mobex account during the month of September, regardless of when you activate your account, will have 100% of your first month's payment go to Pepin Academies.

For over fifteen years, Pepin Academies has helped hundreds of students with learning and related disabilities graduate high school, go to college, and find jobs. For students struggling with disabilities, these are not easy tasks. Like most schools, budgets are tight, so we're teaming up to empower these students with enhanced classroom technology.
